HANDS ON A PRACTICAL TRAINEESHIP

Another point considered in order to apply to this assignment, were the possibilities of making of the assignment a practical one. As I understood from the presentation, this assignment might not be based in Saudí Arabia, due to the lack of housing. Last experience as TSI, has shown me that delivering solutions for our assets on a daily basis, is something I would like to continue doing. This is something that can be developed both on site and from Papendrecht. I also see this as a chance to use the network I developed in OE FM on a Papendrecht based role but, I would obviously miss the practicalities of being on field supervising and doing the jobs needed. But again, is something I am happy to adapt too.

INTEREST ON OPERATIONAL ASPECTS

During my first assignment I had a good assignment developing research on Offshore Fleet operational issues in terms of safety. I see this assignment, as a good opportunity to get this real field experience and grow from the Fleet Management perspective to a more operational profile, that could lead me to better understand and fit in a Boskalis. This is something I pointed out already to HR too, and that may lead to a better fit on this project.

SUMMARY

Although housing in Saudi Arabia is still a challenge and may make of this opportunity a Papendrecht based position, I see my profile adapting to the requirements that may rise on a project like this. Does not matter where it becomes developed, the opportunities that may arise, whether on a Fleet Management or on a more operational side are more than welcome.
